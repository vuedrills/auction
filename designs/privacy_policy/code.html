1. Introduction

Welcome to AirMass. We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our community-based auction platform. By using AirMass, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

To provide our Town-first services, we collect several types of information:

person_pin

Identity Data

Username, profile image, and email address used for account management.

share_location

Precise Location Data

GPS coordinates are collected when you create an auction to validate your presence in the town.

gavel

Transaction Data

Details about auctions you participate in, bids placed, and items listed.

3. Town Visibility

AirMass operates on a hierarchy. While your auctions are created locally within your Home Town, aggregated data (such as total auction volume or highest bids) may be visible at the National level.

"Your specific home location is never exposed publicly. Only your generalized 'Home Town' affiliation is visible to other users."

4. User Rights

You have full control over your data. Under GDPR and CCPA regulations, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Request permanent deletion of your account and associated history.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate profile information directly in settings.
