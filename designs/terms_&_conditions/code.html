1. Introduction

Welcome to AirMass. By accessing our platform, you agree to comply with our community-driven, town-first auction rules. Our mission is to strengthen local economies through transparent, localized bidding.

2. The "Town-First" Rule

home_pin

Local Listings Only

You may only create auctions in your verified home town. This hierarchy ensures genuine local commerce.

While you can browse the national aggregation of auctions, listing privileges are geo-fenced. Attempting to spoof your location to list in other towns is a violation of our core principles and will result in account suspension.
